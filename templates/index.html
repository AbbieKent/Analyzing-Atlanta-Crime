The following heatmap is generated using Tableau.

The majority of reported crime from the Atlanta PD can be seen to focus around the downtown area. Using Tableau, the visualization incorporates over 350,000 crime reports from 2009 to 2020. It is filterable by year in order to see the location of crime change over time.
